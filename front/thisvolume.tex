      {\large\bfseries\color{cmjcrimson}A Short Story}

      \vspace{0.3cm}

      The Mathematics Undergraduate Seminar (UgS) is a tradition of student research and exposition,
      first introduced as part of the MATH programme. This tradition has persisted for years on, 
      until the Spring of 2024. The programme was restructured into an MMDA --- Mathematics, Modeling \& Data Analytics --- variant,
      and the seminar was discontinued.\\[0.3cm]
      As a seminarist myself, this tradition is encoded in Constructor (well, Jacobs...) University's mathematical DNA. 
      A discontinuation in itself would be a step backwards for its mathematics society. So, we relaxed the curricular condition and took the
      seminar outside the classroom. The UgS was therefore reborn as a student-led initiative, attracting a superset of mathematics and non-mathematics students.
      \begin{center}
        \href{https://elshenawyom.github.io/teaching/ugs}{\texttt{elshenawyom.github.io/teaching/ugs}}
      \end{center}
      In its incarnation, the seminar is now a thriving hub of mathematical exploration, bringing students and faculty closer than ever before.  
      This journal is the fruit of this rebirth.
