\section{Conclusion \& Final remarks}
We've followed in the footsteps of one of the greatest revolutionaries that built the foundations of reliable and efficient communication - Claude Shannon. By exploring coding and information theory, we not only built our understanding on a number of underlying concepts but also developed a bird's eye view on how Arikan took the teachings of Shannon to a practical application to the benefit of humanity. The ingenuity and experimentation that Arikan expressed during his development of Polar Codes is an impressive and satisfying learning experience. 

Shannon's Theorem is a beautiful mathematical construct that creates a clear guideline for engineers to build on. Polar Codes are the practical realization of this. They are an incredible tool in the engineer's arsenal that allow optimized communication under the correct circumstances.
